ILYAS 20

The administration has decided to set up Ilyas 20 from this session. with the aim of preparing students for competitive examinations and preparing children for matriculation. Ilyas 20 is not only an educational movement for talented Indian children but also a boon for children who drop out of school due to lack of resources. Under the auspices of Jamia Zakariyya Educational and Welfare Trust, Matriculation as well as INTER students are admitted in Medical, Engineering, CA, CS, Paramedical, CTET, STET, Law and all types of government jobs in future. Will be kept for preparation. Twenty meritorious candidates will be selected every year by Elias 20 who are financially weaker in the society and want to pursue higher education on their own. Before the commencement of the session of Elias 20 Entrance Examination, the administration will issue the announcements of the selection of the children based on its own. Currently, the administration will decide the offline or online exam for the session to start soon and inform the candidates through advertisement.
